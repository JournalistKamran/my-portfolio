Kamran Akhtar

Multimedia Journalist & Documentary Producer

I am an independent journalist and documentary producer with a strong focus on ground reporting, investigative journalism, and policy research. My work blends field investigations with a solution-oriented journalism approach, aiming to highlight systemic issues while also exploring pathways for change. Over the past few years, I have reported from diverse regions across India—from uncovering illegal sand mining operations in Manasbal, Jammu & Kashmir, to exploring the rich tapestry of Kerala’s food, culture, and travel stories. In 2023, I investigated an alleged case of sexual misconduct involving a student from RIT College during the RITU festival in Kottayam. Despite inconsistencies in the FIR, media narratives, and hospital records, I chose not to publish the story due to safety concerns surrounding the survivor—an editorial decision grounded in ethics and empathy. I am committed to storytelling that informs, empowers, and drives impact. If you're looking for a journalist who believes in research-driven narratives, ethical storytelling, and deep on-the-ground reporting, you’re at the right place.
